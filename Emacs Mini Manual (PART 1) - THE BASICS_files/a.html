                                  Hi,

                                  One detail:

                                  (add-hook 'c-mode-common-hook
                                  (lambda ()
                                  (when (derived-mode-p 'c-mode 'c++-mode 'java-mode 'asm-mode)
                                  (ggtags-mode 1))))

                                  did not work for me for assembly.
                                  I replaced it by:

                                  (add-hook 'c-mode-common-hook
                                  (lambda ()
                                  (when (derived-mode-p 'c-mode 'c++-mode)
                                  (ggtags-mode 1))))
                                  (add-hook 'asm-mode-hook
                                  (lambda ()
                                  (ggtags-mode 1)))

                                  which works for me (I don't program in Java, by the way...).
